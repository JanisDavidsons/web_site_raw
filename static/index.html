About Codelex!

17th of february 2020
  Two month ago I made decition to change my career to Information technology as programmer. Thanks to my brother, I came across amazing school called Codelex. They were about to launch three days programming course in the end of january, so I applied for it. I was amazed about exercises they offered. In fact - three of those were actually games. Those games were prepared only as sceleton that we had to build upon. Basicly we had to implement game logic. Now that was extremely engaging! I would like to share those with you. Please remember, I`m actualy learning on the fly, which means - you might find bugs on this website and also in those games. I have very poor programming experience, all of this is achieved by by A LOT of work.
